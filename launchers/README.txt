Can be installed under ~/.local/share/applications either by copying or symlink.
For global stuff, you can copy under /usr/share/applications.

Examples:
    ln -s ~/my-home/launchers/ipython-qtconsole-anaconda2.desktop ~/.local/share/applications/ipython-qtconsole-anaconda2.desktop
    ln -s ~/my-home/launchers/ipython-qtconsole.desktop           ~/.local/share/applications/ipython-qtconsole.desktop


    sudo cp visualvm-oracle-jdk8.desktop /usr/share/applications/
